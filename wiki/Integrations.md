# Integrations

`lotus-idea` is an orchestrating domain service. It must integrate through
source-owned APIs, data products, and Gateway/BFF contracts.

## Upstream

1. `lotus-core`
2. `lotus-performance`
3. `lotus-risk`
4. `lotus-advise`
5. `lotus-manage`
6. `lotus-report`
7. `lotus-ai`

## Downstream

1. `lotus-gateway`
2. `lotus-workbench`
3. `lotus-advise`
4. `lotus-manage`
5. `lotus-report`
6. `lotus-render`
7. `lotus-archive`

Integration claims are planned until the relevant RFC-0002 slice is implemented
and certified.
